Sejare's Homemade Lasagna

This homemade lasagna is sure to be a crowd pleaser!

This lasagna recipe has been handed down generation to generation, working its way all the back to a little town in Italy called Alberobello! It is a simple, classic version of an Italian staple.

Ingredients

  • One large jar of your favorite tomato sauce
  • One box of lasagna noodles
  • Mozzeralla Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Ritcotta Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Start boiling salted water
  2. Combine the cheeses in a large mixing bowl along with eggs and salt and pepper
  3. Boil lasagna noodles until underdone
  4. Layer tomato sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture until you run out of noodles
  5. Bake on 400 *F for 30 minutes, then sprinkle desired amount of Mozzeralla cheese on top, bake for 15 more minutes or until cheese is nicely melted
  6. Let cool for 20 minutes before serving
  7. Enjoy!
